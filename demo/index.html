TheSwitch

One config. Full atmosphere.

Not just dark mode — a whole mood engine. Pick an atmosphere and the palette, gradient, glow, the floating HUD and the live ambient canvas all change together, through a cinematic transition. 

TheSwitch.create({
  defaultSkin: "midnight",
  ambient: true,
  transition: "portal",
  autoBind: true,
});
