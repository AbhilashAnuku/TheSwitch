TheSwitch

The theme that matches the moment.

This whole page is styled only with TheSwitch's --ts-* variables and [data-atmos-skin] selectors. Change the mode, opt into live weather, or pick a skin below — everything you see restyles itself.

Mode

Auto lets TheSwitch choose from the moment (time, season and, if you opt in, the sky). Light and Dark pin the base.

Live weather (opt-in)

Off by default — the page makes zero network calls and themes purely from your clock and hemisphere. Turn it on to ask for location and fetch current conditions from Open-Meteo.

Manual skin override

Force any skin to preview its preset — try sunny, watery, snow or night. Choose “Auto” to hand control back to the atmosphere engine.
