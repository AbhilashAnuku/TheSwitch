# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our standards
Be respectful, welcoming, and constructive. Examples of unacceptable behavior:
harassment, insults or derogatory comments, trolling, and publishing others'
private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that
violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any
contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate. To report a concern, contact
the maintainer privately (see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the private channel).

This is a condensed adaptation of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), v2.1.
